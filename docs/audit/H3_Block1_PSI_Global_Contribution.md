## 2. Structural Observations

When the 12 PSI sequence features are appended to the 12 spatial/load features, the Effective Rank of the population explodes to **16.36**.

This indicates that PSI responses (Entropy, Autocorrelation, Slope) are **largely orthogonal** (mathematically independent) to the spatial baseline and load transitions. If PSI parameters were strongly driving the absolute reaction times, the Eff.Rank would not have expanded nearly exactly by the number of added dimensions.

## Formal Verdict

> **PSI_MODERATE_CONTRIBUTION** (Orthogonal)

PSI is a major independent reservoir of population variance, but it does not collapse or explain away the baseline spatial geometry; rather, it operates as a parallel structural domain.
